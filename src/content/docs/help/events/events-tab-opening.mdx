#####  Cntrol what action will open new tab, and if the new tab get the focus (select)

<br/>

<Image class="help-img" src={eventsTabOpening} alt="image" />

<div class="topic">
  Open tabs from:
  <ul class="list-disc pl-8">
    <li>Bookmarks</li>
    <li>Groups of bookmarks</li>
    <aside aria-label="Note" class="starlight-aside starlight-aside--note">
      When checked "Open All in Tabs" will open the bookmarks in new tabs, when unchecked the
      bookmarks will replace all tabs that are not locked or protected.
    </aside>
    <li>History</li>
    <li>Address bar</li>
    <li>Search bar</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="topic">
  <ul class="list-disc pl-8">
    <li>
      Middle-click or Control-click opens items in current tab<br />Only for bookmarks,
      history, links forced to open in new tab
    </li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="topic">
  Focus/Select tabs that open by these action:
  <ul class="list-disc pl-8">
    <li>Links</li>
    <li>Diverted windows</li>
    <li>Other applications</li>
    <li>New tab commands</li>
    <li>Duplicate Tab</li>
    <li>Address bar</li>
    <li>Search bar</li>
    <li>Context menu search for</li>
    <li>Bookmarks/History</li>
    <li>Groups of bookmarks</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="topic">
  Inverse Middle-click or Control-click focus for tabs opened by these action:
  <ul class="list-disc pl-8">
    <li>Links</li>
    <li>Bookmarks/History, Address bar, Search bar</li>
  </ul>
</div>
